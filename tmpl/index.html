Hello {{.Username}}, Welcome To Go Notes!

Go Notes is a simple webserver that is built in golang for storing all your random thoughts during the day or just a reminder to do something. We are not here to do anything fancy, but maybe we will reconsider in the future.

As you build out your collection of notes, you will see a list of them populated here on the home page. You can come back here at any time by clicking the Go Notes logo in the top left hand corner of the application.

Don't worry if this screen looks different next time you come back here, you've just started to get the hang of things.

Creating New Notes

Is very easy!

Follow the steps below to create your first note. You can name it whatever you want.

Browse to http://localhost:4646/edit/Name
Fill in the information for your note and click Save
You have created your first note!
